Game controls
Welcome to our exciting Defender game!Take command of a powerful
spaceship and protect your world from relentless waves of enemy attackers. 
Your mission is to Obliterate the threats and save humanoids while safeguarding
vital structures. Engage in fast paced action and navigate through the landscape 
exhibiting your strategic prowess and pinpoint accuracy in DEFENSE.
Use the Arrow keys to navigate the spaceship through the terrain, Spacebar 
to fire missiles, S to activate the temporal shield and P to pause and unpause.
